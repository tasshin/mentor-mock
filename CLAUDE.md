## Project Vision

Creating an ideal mentor using AI, documents, and code.

Not a single mentor, but a **constellation of mentors** — each calibrated to different domains and contexts. The system should deeply understand you (person-mentor fit) and be able to invoke different perspectives (the Rubik's Cube metaphor — rotating in any mentor as needed).

## Core Themes

- **Multiplicity**: Many mentors, each specialized
- **Rotating perspectives**: Invoke different heroes/mental models for different situations
- **Person-mentor fit**: Deeply calibrated to you specifically
- **Understanding as they understand themselves**: Grasping your internal logic
- **Bi-directional**: Help you understand yourself as both mentee and mentor
- **Learning as priority**: Continuous curiosity and growth
